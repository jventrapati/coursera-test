Rice Cooking Steps(Using Ordered Lists)
  1. Take a cup of rice grains in a bowl
  2. Wash the rice
  3. Add two cups of water to the rice
  4. Switch on the Instant Pot
    1. Set the mode to Rice
    2. Put the bowl of rice with water in the pot
    3. Place the lid
    4. Press Rice on the instant pot display
